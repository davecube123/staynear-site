StayNear (“we,” “our,” or “us”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our app works and what information we handle.

The StayNear app does not collect personal data such as names, email addresses, or phone numbers. The app only processes search queries (such as location and radius) to help you find hotels near landmarks.

StayNear uses affiliate links to Booking.com. When you click a booking button, you will be redirected to Booking.com, where their terms and privacy policy apply.

We do not store your personal information. Any hotel data shown in the app is provided by third-party providers (e.g., Google Places or Booking.com).
