Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025-09-23

Overview

StayNear helps you find hotels near any landmark in the Philippines. We keep data collection to the minimum necessary to provide core functionality.

Data We Process

  • Location: If you allow location, we use it to position the map and calculate distances. Location data is not stored on our servers.
  • Analytics: We may collect anonymized usage events (e.g., screen views, button taps) to improve the app.
  • Outbound Links: When you tap a hotel or activity, you are redirected to our partner (e.g., Agoda or Klook) to complete your booking. Your use of partner sites is governed by their policies.

Third-Party Services

  • Maps: Google Maps SDK.
  • Affiliate Partners: Agoda, Klook (and others we may add in the future).

Your Choices

  • You can disable location permissions in your device settings.
  • You can opt out of analytics if we expose a toggle in Settings (coming soon).
