Hotels near any landmark — Philippines-wide

Pick a landmark, set a radius, and instantly see nearby stays. Clean, map-first, and built for travelers in the Philippines.

Beta: StayNear is currently in closed beta and launching publicly on Google Play soon. Traffic will come from the mobile app and this landing page.

Why StayNear?

Landmark → Radius

Choose any point on the map or a named landmark. Drag to set your search radius and we’ll surface stays closest to that spot.

Fast & Focused

Noise-free results sorted by distance. Optional ratings and photos to help you decide.

PH-wide Coverage

From island destinations like Siargao to city hubs like BGC—if it’s on the map, you can search around it.

About the App

  • Platform: Android (Kotlin, Material 3)
  • Map: Google Maps
  • Discovery: OpenStreetMap/Overpass (by coordinates + radius)
  • Extra signals (optional): Google Places ratings/photos; price probes when dates are provided
  • Monetization: Affiliate deep links (Agoda now; activities partner coming soon)

StayNear keeps the list brand-neutral. When you tap a stay, we open our partner in a secure browser tab to complete your booking.
